Privacy and bank-data notice

Effective July 14, 2026. Household CFO Method uses Plaid only when you choose to connect a financial institution.

What we retrieve

We retrieve account names, partial account numbers, balances, and transaction history needed to help you review spending and maintain your household plan. We do not receive or store your bank username or password and we do not initiate payments or move money.

How decisions work

Synced activity stays separate from confirmed actuals. Pending charges are informational. Posted expenses become draft reviews only when you select them, and they affect actuals only after you confirm.

Storage and sharing

Plaid access credentials are encrypted and kept only on the server. Raw synced accounts and transactions are not sent to Mia or external AI providers; a transaction summary can enter Mia's supervised context only after you select it for a draft. We use service providers only to operate and secure the application and do not sell bank data.

Your choices

You can disconnect an institution at any time. Disconnecting asks Plaid to remove the Item, removes the stored access credential and unapproved Plaid source data, and keeps financial records you already approved.
